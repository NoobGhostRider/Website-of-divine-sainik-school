Welcome To Divine Sainik School

Divine Sainik School is a creative temple of learning was established from well being blessing, command and revelation of spiritual master Shri-Shri 108 Swami Bhagwat Das Ji Maharaj who was late prior of sacred grove Nakhighat Ashram , dweller of Saket Dham, in 2003 with a view to impart modern, scientific and cultural compatible education to children. It is situated near birth place of Saint Kabirdas at the road of Lahartara via D.L.W .

Infrastructure wise, the school is best equipped having the highest number of computers in its lab. The music faculty has its own sound proof practice areas.
